About Kodaikanal Campus

Nestled amidst the captivating hills of Kodaikanal, the Kodaikanal Center stands as the crown jewel of Mother Teresa Women’s University, situated in the tranquil locality of Attuvampatty. Surrounded by the breathtaking natural beauty of Kodaikanal, characterized by lush greenery and a cool climate, the center provides a picturesque backdrop for academic activities. The scenic splendor of the hilly region contributes to a serene atmosphere, fostering an ideal environment for learning and research. Beyond its aesthetic appeal, the Kodaikanal Center plays a pivotal role in the university's academic and administrative functions, serving as a central hub for students and faculty alike. This academic oasis offers students a unique and inspiring backdrop for their educational journey, where the harmony between nature and academia creates a transformative and enriching experience. The center, with its strategic location and symbiotic connection with Kodaikanal's natural surroundings, encapsulates the essence of holistic education and the university's commitment to providing a conducive and inspiring learning environment.

Facilities Available

  • 24/7 hot water facilities available in every department, laboratory, and on-campus housing.
  • Specialized and well-equipped laboratories in various departments for conducting research and experiments.
  • Dedicated computer labs with high-speed internet, printers, and other resources to support students' computing needs.
  • Modern and comfortable classrooms and lecture halls equipped with audio-visual facilities for effective teaching and learning.
  • Comprehensive library system providing access to an extensive collection of books, journals, and educational resources.
  • Sports and recreational facilities, including gyms, swimming pools, sports fields, and courts, promoting physical fitness and wellness among students and staff.
  • On-campus housing with comfortable accommodations and dining facilities, ensuring a safe and inclusive living environment for students.
  • Health and wellness services, including medical clinics, counseling centers, and wellness programs, to support the physical and mental well-being of students and staff.
  • Career services offering job fairs, resume workshops, and career counseling to help students and graduates prepare for and find employment.
  • Yoga center promoting holistic well-being and mindfulness among students and staff.
  • Fitness facilities for promoting a healthy lifestyle, including exercise equipment and wellness programs.
  • Transportation services with buses facilitating convenient commuting for students to and from the university campus.
  • Facilities for all departments to have their own dedicated labs, creating specialized spaces for academic and research activities.
  • Committed and highly qualified faculty, with the majority holding doctoral degrees in their respective fields.
  • An esteemed academic staff dedicated to providing students with a rich and intellectually stimulating learning experience.
  • A faculty team with diverse educational backgrounds, fostering a multidisciplinary approach to education and research.
  • Professors and instructors who are not only experts in their fields but also passionate about imparting knowledge and nurturing academic growth.
  • An environment where all staff members are actively engaged in continuous professional development, ensuring they stay abreast of the latest advancements in their disciplines.
  • A culture that values and encourages research, with faculty members actively contributing to the academic community through publications, conferences, and research projects.
  • A team of educators who are dedicated to mentoring and guiding students, fostering an atmosphere of intellectual curiosity and critical thinking.

    About Coimbatore Campus

    The Mother Teresa Women’s University Research and Extension Centre in Coimbatore, established in July 2016, holds a pivotal position within the vibrant academic landscape of the city. Housed in the prominent Coimbatore Corporation building at No.16, Arokiaswamy Road (West), R.S Puram, Coimbatore, India, the center was inaugurated with great enthusiasm and fervor. The auspicious occasion witnessed the presence of distinguished dignitaries, including Thiru.K.P.Anbalagan, Minister for Higher Education, and Thiru.S.P.Velumani, Minister for Municipal Administration, Rural Development, and Implementation of Special Programs, who jointly marked the commencement of this academic endeavor on 25.08.2016. Dedicated to extending its services to women students of diverse communities, the centre has evolved as a beacon of educational empowerment. The initial focus of the center was on providing Post Graduate courses in key disciplines such as Commerce, Mathematics, Textiles and Clothing, Foods and Nutrition, and English. Additionally, the center embraced a robust research agenda with M.Phil and Ph.D. programs, emphasizing the importance of advanced academic pursuits. With a holistic vision of empowering women across all spheres of society, the Mother Teresa Women’s University Research and Extension Centre in Coimbatore actively engages in the triad of Education, Research, and Training. The multifaceted approach aims not only to impart knowledge but also to foster a culture of continuous learning, critical inquiry, and scholarly exploration. As the center continues to evolve, it stands as a testament to the university's commitment to advancing the status of women in academia and beyond, shaping future leaders and contributors to societal progress.
