About

Established in 2006, the Department of Chemistry, Mother Teresa Women’s University, Kodaikanal is motivated to provide a complete learning opportunity and quality education encompassing developments in frontier research areas in chemistry. We aim to strongly motivate our students for research and provide them adequate training in synthesis, characterization, application studies and instrumentation and equip students to meet the global requisites for employment. The Department offers M. Sc., M. Phil., and Ph. D programs. The Department is specialized in research areas such as Coordination Chemistry, X-ray- crystallography, Medicinal Chemistry and Bioinorganic Chemistry

Mission
  • Advance scientific knowledge in the field of chemistry through rigorous research and inquiry.
  • Provide high-quality education and training to students, fostering a deep understanding of chemical principles.
  • Promote interdisciplinary collaboration to address complex challenges and contribute to scientific advancements.
  • Engage in community outreach and public education to enhance awareness and appreciation for chemistry.
  • Encourage ethical conduct, integrity, and responsibility in all aspects of research and education.
  • Strive for excellence in teaching, research, and service to positively impact society.
    • Vision
  • Be a globally recognized center of excellence in chemical research and education.
  • Lead innovations in the field of chemistry that contribute to sustainable development and societal well-being.
  • Cultivate a diverse and inclusive academic community that nurtures creativity and fosters collaboration.
  • Produce graduates who are well-equipped with the knowledge and skills to excel in diverse careers.
  • Establish strong partnerships with industry, academia, and research institutions for mutually beneficial collaborations.
  • Continuously adapt and evolve to stay at the forefront of emerging trends and technologies in chemistry.
