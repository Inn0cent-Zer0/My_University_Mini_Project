About

The Primary Purpose of the Home Science Department is to provide opportunities for students to pursue a quality education in Home Science. Importance is placed on providing opportunities within the curricula for development of enhanced skills in critical thinking, communication, leadership and computer literacy. The Department of Home Science also seeks to provide students opportunities for growth beyond the classroom through a wide range of extracurricular activities, programmes and services through the maintenance of environment, cultural and intellectual diversity. The Courses offered under the Department of Home Science prevails in all three Research and Extension centres such as Madurai, Chennai and Coimbatore seeking opportunities for developing Academic Excellence, the students has more scope to get the exposure for research, projects, internship, industrial visit, and placement.

Mission
  • Empower individuals with practical skills and knowledge in home science, promoting overall well-being and sustainable living.
  • Provide transformative education that equips students with the expertise to address contemporary challenges in home management, nutrition, and family dynamics.
  • Foster critical thinking by encouraging students to analyze and apply scientific principles to everyday life situations.
  • Promote community engagement and outreach initiatives that enhance the quality of life for individuals and families.
  • Support research endeavors that contribute to advancements in home science, benefiting society at large.
    • Vision
  • Aspire to be a renowned Home Science department, acknowledged for excellence in education, research, and community impact.
  • Cultivate a community of scholars and students committed to exploring innovative solutions for contemporary challenges in home science.
  • Create a dynamic learning environment that values inclusivity, diversity, and the integration of emerging technologies in home science education.
  • Strive for global recognition as a hub for cutting-edge research and practices in home science, contributing to the advancement of the field.
